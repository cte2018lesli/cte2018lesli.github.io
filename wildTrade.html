The wild trade

The harmful "Wild Trade Cycle" continues to endanger animals.
Including the most iconic animals we grow up learning about:
Rhinos, Elephants, Tigers and Bears

The cycle starts with the desire for a rare exotic animal....

Hunters go after the rarest of animals to earn more money

More hunters capturing rare animals --> the species population continues to decrease -->
the species becomes even rarer
Resulting in a bigger demand for a species on the brink of extinction...
